Privacy Policy

Sheeza Manzil Guesthouse
Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you make a booking through our website, we collect:

  • Full name and contact details (phone number)
  • Nationality
  • A copy of your ID card or passport
  • Payment slip (if paying by bank transfer)
  • Booking details (room, dates, number of guests)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Process and manage your booking
  • Communicate with you about your reservation (via WhatsApp)
  • Comply with Maldivian tourism regulations requiring guest ID verification
  • Generate invoices and receipts

3. How We Store Your Information

Your booking details and uploaded documents are stored securely in our booking management system. Documents (ID cards, payment slips) are stored in encrypted cloud storage and are only accessible to authorised guesthouse staff.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except where required by Maldivian law (e.g. tourism authority regulations).

5. Data Retention

We retain guest records for as long as required by applicable regulations. You may contact us to request deletion of your data after your stay.
